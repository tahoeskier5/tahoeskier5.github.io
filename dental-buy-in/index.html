Model notes: Practice value and annual profit both grow by the CAGR input. Ownership value = practice value × ownership percentage. Net buy-in = original ownership value − sweat equity credit. Positive monthly cash flow is calculated after the scheduled loan payment. When the sweep option is enabled, positive monthly cash flow is immediately applied as extra principal until the loan is paid off. Debt-adjusted position excluding equity = cumulative distributions − cumulative payments − remaining loan balance. Total position including equity = debt-adjusted position excluding equity + current estimated ownership value.
